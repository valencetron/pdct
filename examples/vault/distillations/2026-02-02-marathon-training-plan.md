# Marathon Training Plan

Ada committed to the May marathon. The assistant drafted a 16-week block: base phase (weeks 1-6), build (7-12), peak (13-14), taper (15-16). Long runs progress 12→22 miles. Two strength sessions weekly to protect the left knee, which flared during last year's half.

## Key Points
- 16-week block, long run peaks at 22 miles in week 14
- Left knee history: strength work is non-negotiable
- Weekly mileage capped at +10% per week
