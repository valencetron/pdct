# Sourdough Hydration Experiments

Ada ran three weekend bakes at 70%, 78%, and 85% hydration with the same starter. The 78% loaf had the best crumb structure and oven spring. The 85% dough was unworkable without lamination technique. Cold-proofing 14 hours at 4°C improved flavor in all three.

## Key Points
- 78% hydration is the keeper recipe
- 85% requires lamination; parked until technique improves
- 14h cold proof at 4°C consistently improves flavor
