# Spring Garden Planting Schedule

Ada mapped the three raised beds: bed one gets tomatoes (Sun Gold and Brandywine), bed two peppers and eggplant, bed three herbs and lettuce succession plantings every two weeks. Last frost date is April 15; tomato starts go out May 1 with wall-o-water protection.

## Key Points
- Sun Gold and Brandywine tomatoes in bed one, out May 1
- Lettuce succession every two weeks in bed three
- Drip irrigation timer set to 20 minutes at dawn
